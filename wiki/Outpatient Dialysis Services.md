# US Outpatient Dialysis Infrastructure and Medicare Policy

## Exam Logic
Questions on dialysis delivery usually target the **shift in modalities** (In-center $\rightarrow$ Home) and the **economic landscape** of ESRD care. 

**Common Distractors:**
*   **Facility Type:** Confusing "Hospital-based" with "Freestanding." In the US, the vast majority ($\approx$96%) of FFS treatments occur in **freestanding** facilities, not hospitals.
*   **Insurance Trends:** Assuming FFS Medicare remains the dominant payer. The **21st Century Cures Act** triggered a massive shift toward **Medicare Advantage (MA)**; as of 2023, the split is roughly 50/50.
*   **Home Dialysis Equity:** A common pitfall is assuming home dialysis uptake is uniform. There is a significant **racial disparity**: Black patients are underrepresented in home dialysis (23% of home users vs. 29% of total FFS ESRD population).

**Conceptual Pitfalls:**
*   **Drug Utilization:** Do not assume ESA or Vitamin D use is static. There has been a sharp $\downarrow$ in per-treatment use of ESAs ($\downarrow$61%) and Vitamin D ($\downarrow$73%) since 2010, reflecting updated guidelines to avoid over-correction of anemia and mineral bone disease.

## Core Technical Summary

### 1. Market Structure & Delivery
*   **Concentration:** Highly consolidated. Two Large Dialysis Organizations (LDOs)тАФ**DaVita and Fresenius**тАФcontrol $\approx$75% of facilities and treatments.
*   **Facility Distribution:** 
    *   **Freestanding:** 96% of treatments.
    *   **Hospital-based:** 4% of treatments (declining faster than freestanding).
*   **Ownership:** 90% for-profit.

### 2. Payer Shift (FFS $\rightarrow$ MA)
*   **Driver:** 21st Century Cures Act (permitted dialysis patients to enroll in MA).
*   **Trend:** FFS share dropped from $\approx$80% (2019) $\rightarrow$ $\approx$50% (2023).

### 3. Modality Trends
*   **Home Dialysis:** Steady $\uparrow$ in prevalence (9% in 2011 $\rightarrow$ 17% in 2023).
*   **Access:** $\approx$53% of facilities now offer home dialysis services.

### 4. Pharmacologic Trends (2010 $\rightarrow$ 2023)
Significant $\downarrow$ in per-treatment utilization:
*   **ESAs:** $\downarrow$61%
*   **Vitamin D agents:** $\downarrow$73%
*   **Iron agents:** $\downarrow$24%
*   **Other drugs:** $\downarrow$85%

### 5. Economic Impact
*   **Total Cost:** Annual healthcare expense per dialysis beneficiary $\approx$ \$102,000.
*   **Marginal Profit:** FFS Medicare marginal profit was $\approx$17% in 2023.
