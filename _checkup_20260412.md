## 9. Recommendations

1. **LaTeX cleanup script**: `sed -i '' 's/\$\\rightarrow\$/→/g; s/\$\\ge\$/≥/g; ...'` across all wiki/*.md. One-time fix + add post-processing to worker.
2. **Worker crash resilience**: Add exponential backoff on connection errors. After 3 consecutive failures, sleep 5min before retry. Skip orphan after 2 failures in same cycle.
3. **Implement editorial review**: Even a lightweight pass (Sonnet, not Opus) checking LaTeX, missing fields, and section structure would catch issues #1, #4, #7, #8.
4. **Rotate worker log**: Archive `_worker_log.md` weekly (zip to `_archive/`). Keep current week only.
5. **Backfill `source:` field**: For 15 legacy files, reverse-lookup from vault raw.md to populate source.
6. **Implement sync_canonical_updates()**: When vault wiki updates, push delta to repo. Currently manual.
7. **Filter garbled orphan titles**: Add title validation (min length, ASCII ratio) before attempting wikification.
